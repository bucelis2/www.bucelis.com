Non Stick Pan

This pan was expertly crafted with e-commerce in mind, designed to be both eye-catching and highly clickable. Alongside the pan, I also designed a matching lid and set of utensils, which contributed to the productʼs strong sales performance in both the United States and Germany. The end result was a product that not only stood out online but also delivered exceptional functionality.

Pinocchio

Pinocchio was my first design project after graduating from the Academy of Arts — and it continues to travel the world, featured in exhibitions to this day. It’s a modular chair with a playful twist: it stands on only two legs and gains stability only when connected to another chair. Pinocchio is more than just a piece of furniture — it’s a poetic exploration of balance, connection, and the idea that we are stronger together.
